About the Club

FC Barcelona, also known as Barça, is one of the most successful and popular football clubs in the world. Founded in 1899, the club is based in Barcelona, Catalonia, Spain. Known for their attacking style of play and youth academy "La Masia", Barça has produced legends like Lionel Messi, Xavi, Iniesta, and Ronaldinho.

Major Achievements

  • La Liga Titles: 27
  • UEFA Champions League Titles: 5
  • Copa del Rey: 31
  • FIFA Club World Cup: 3
  • UEFA Super Cup: 5

Camp Nou Stadium

The iconic Camp Nou is the home stadium of FC Barcelona. It has a seating capacity of over 99,000, making it the largest stadium in Europe.
